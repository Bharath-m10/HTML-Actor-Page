Vijay

**Vijay**, popularly known as **Thalapathy Vijay**, is a leading actor in Tamil cinema and one of the most influential stars in South India. Born as **Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar** on **June 22, 1974**, in Chennai, he is the son of director S. A. Chandrasekhar and playback singer Shoba Chandrasekhar. Vijay began his acting career as a child artist and made his debut as a lead actor in the 1992 film *Naalaiya Theerpu*. Over the years, he gained massive popularity with blockbuster films like *Ghilli*, *Thuppakki*, *Mersal*, *Master*, and *Leo*. Known for his charismatic screen presence, powerful performances, and energetic dance moves, Vijay has become a household name. Apart from acting, he is also a playback singer, with hits like “Google Google” and “Vaathi Coming.” He is deeply admired for his humility and philanthropic work through his fan club *Vijay Makkal Iyakkam*, which engages in various social welfare activities. In 2024, he founded his political party named **Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK)**, indicating a shift toward active politics with the aim of serving people beyond cinema. With a massive fan base across Tamil Nadu and among the global Tamil diaspora, Vijay continues to be a powerful force both on and off the screen.
